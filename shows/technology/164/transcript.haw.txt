Ellie i nā huahana hua'ōlelo e nānā i ka mea e hiki mai ana
i ka hebedoma i ko mākou hoʻolauleʻaʻana i kā mākou 165 whoa
ka hopena aʻaʻole wau e hoʻomanaʻo
ma ka hōʻike waleʻana i ka hōʻike weliweli
ka mea, e hiki e aku weliweli ina oe e
maʻaneʻi,ʻaʻoleʻoe e kala aku i ka hoʻolaha
ʻeā, mai'ōleloʻoe iā mākou hiki iaʻu ke'ōlelo weliweli
ʻAʻoleʻo ia he wiki nui loa
ʻike nā mea a pau iā ia
